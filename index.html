Why is Blood Donation Important?

Imagine having the power to save three lives with just one simple act. Every day, countless lives hang in the balance, waiting for a miracle that could be as simple as a single blood donation. Yet, nearly 12,000 people lose their lives each year due to the lack of quality blood. But you can make a difference.

By donating just 450-500 ml of blood, you have the potential to become a real-life hero, giving hope to those in desperate need. Understanding this critical need, IIT Bhubaneswar is proud to present Umeed 4.0—a lifesaving Blood Donation Camp.

we organise Umeed 4.0 this year, we aim to not only save lives but also to inspire a deep sense of social responsibility. Umeed is more than just a blood donation camp; it’s a movement that encourages everyone to become repeat donors, making lifesaving a habit.
